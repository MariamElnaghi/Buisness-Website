Welcome to FurBalls Bunny care Guide

    Instructions:
  • Feed your Bunny regular and scheduled appointments (examples of bunny food below)
    1. Parsley
    2. Romanian Lettuce (Warning: Don't feed Iceburg Lettuce)
    3. Mint
    4. Cucumber
    5. Tomatoes
    6. Grapes (As treats only, once a day maximum)

  • Stay alert for any changes in your pet's actions
    1. How active they are(See if they are less active throughout the day, if they are, contact your vet)
    2. Eating habits (Look out if they reject their food and if they do offer them their favorite food and if they reject it contact your vet, if they don't then they are just being picky)
    3. Their litter box (Check how much times they use it and if decreases contact your vet)

  • Avoid picking up your pet flipping it on its back
    because it makes them think they are caught by a predator

  • Avoid bathing your bunny because it is
    dangerous for it and it might catch a cold

  • Brush your bunny's furry

  • Do not stress your bunny, causes of stress for bunnies:
    1. Dogs / Cats
    2. New places / people
    3. Picking it up or flipping it on the back

  • It is better if you don't use a harness for your pet and also avoid dressing it up
